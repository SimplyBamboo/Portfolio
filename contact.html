To help me understand your project and provide an accurate quote faster, please include the following details when you contact me:

  • Project Type: Building, Modeling, or both?
  • Description: What exactly do you need created? Be specific!
  • Style: What aesthetic are you going for (e.g., Low Poly, Realistic, Cartoony, Sci-Fi)?
  • Size/Scale: Approximate dimensions or scope (e.g., small prop, medium map section, full large map).
  • Quality/Detail Level: Basic, Standard, or High Detail? (Refer to Pricing page for examples).
  • Textures/Materials (Optional): Do you need basic colors, simple textures, or complex PBR materials?
  • References (Optional): Provide any concept art, images, or links that illustrate what you want.
  • Deadline (Optional): Do you have a specific timeframe?
  • Budget (Optional): Knowing your budget helps me tell you what I can do for you.
